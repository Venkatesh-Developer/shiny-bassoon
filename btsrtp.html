Crate & Barrel offers a variety of "upmarket" housewares, furniture, and related merchandise. These are displayed in the "vignette" style, where items are grouped together as they might appear in the home. The company was an innovator of this style, which has since become more widely used.Crate & Barrel has two sister brands. CB2 is a home furnishings division geared toward young adults[29] created in 2000.[30][31] The brand, open since 2000, now has 15 stores across the U.S. and Canada. An additional store is planned to open in Denver later this year.Crate and Barrel's kids brand The Land of Nod, acquired in 2001, plans to close all stores by the end of Spring 2018 and will become Crate&Kids, a kids furnishings, home goods and gifts product offering launching online and in 40 stores starting March 7.
